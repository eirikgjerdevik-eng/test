How to play

Find words using only the 7 letters shown. Each row starts with a different letter — fill in a 4, 5, 6, or 7-letter word for each cell.

All letters in your word must come from the available set (repeats allowed). Grey cells mean no valid word exists for that slot.

Hover over a cell and click ? to reveal a letter as a hint.
